About me

I am a self learned front-end developer, travel guide book writer from Taipei, Taiwan.

I graduated from History department of National Taiwan Normal University. After that joined the work & travel project in United States as a lifeguard. I have become a travel lover since then.

I made my first contact with travel industry in National Palace Museum during my 1-year military surving as a group guide.

I joined Cite Publishing Ltd. as an editor in the beginning, soon after i became a travel guide book writer&editor. I was fully in charge of product design and execution. I published 11 travel guide books, 8 them sold out first copy, 3 of them sold out second copy. Completed oversea expeditions over more than 10 countries. I also supported other teams for writing magazine columns, copywriting on social media and making videos on Youtube chanel. I always enjoy learning new things and being a reliable teammate.

Recently I started to learn front-end development. I learned everything online by myself.
